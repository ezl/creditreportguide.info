Credit Score

The applicant's FICO score is a measure of his or her creditworthiness.

It's traditionally used to determine someone's likelhood to repay a debt like a mortgage or a car loan, but is commonly used in the rental industry for helping determine if an applicant is likely to pay rent on time.

Scores range from 350-850. Anything over a 700 is considered excellent credit worthiness in most states.
